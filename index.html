Some things I want to say

Silent sounds are the most deafening.

From a class, I like photography. The teacher said the picture I took was too rigid， so I wanted to prove something.Slowly in the high-paced learning life, I really like to be quiet in the world of video.

When I was out of breath, I took the camera to discover the beauty and bring peace to my heart.

I like to take black-and-white images best, and then post-process them to create abstract, beautiful, and colorful shapes. At the same time, photography has brought me a lot of design inspiration and expression desire.

Because of the epidemic, I am very worried and can only contact the world through the Internet every day. However, small phones are connected to huge information, and people will inevitably fall into such anxiety. The torrent of information spewed out at once. Is the so-called truth really a fact?

In January, rows of trees in the winter touched me. Some trees are lonely between the two buildings, so helpless. Some trees grow hard towards the sky, as if the clouds are the soil. Some trees quietly looked down on humans at high places. Nature is us, we are nature. So I took this picture that impressed me and put it in my own little website.If these photos can bring you a touch, I would appreciate it.
